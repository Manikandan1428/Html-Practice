Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United States?
  • Tacos first appeared in the United States in the early 20th century.
  • They were introduced by Mexican immigrants who brought their cuisine with them.
  • The earliest records of tacos in the U.S. date back to around 1905.
  • Mexican workers in the U.S. helped spread tacos in southern states, particularly in Texas and California.
  • Fast-food chains like Taco Bell, founded in 1962, popularized tacos nationwide.
  • By the 1970s, tacos had become a mainstream American food, enjoyed across diverse communities
